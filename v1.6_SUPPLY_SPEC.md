# v1.6 Supply Update Specification

## Core Concept
Supply is no longer a simple radius; it is a network. Expanding supply via roads, depots, trucks, and ships is central to victory.

### 1. Supply Expansion Logic
- **Default Radius**: Reduced (TBD).
- **HQ**: Primary source of supply.
- **Roads**: Expand supply from HQ. 
  - **Dirt Roads (Tier 1)**: Extend supply only 2 tiles perpendicular/off the road.
- **Supply Depots**: Provide supply on isolated road networks not connected to HQ.
  - Radius: 2 tiles.

### 2. Road Construction Timing
Construction time now depends on terrain:
- **Plains / Light Forest**: 1 turn
- **Dense Forest / Hills**: 2 turns
- **Mountains**: 6 turns

### 3. New Units
#### Supply Trucks
- **Fragility**: Very fragile.
- **Movement**: Fast on roads, very slow off-road. Cannot enter Dense Forest or Mountains without roads. (Mountains are still 1 tile/turn even with roads).
- **UI**: Unit info panel (bottom left) must show supply range (Base Tier 0 = 1 tile).
- **Progression**: Researchable upgrades for supply range and chassis.

#### Supply Ships
- **Progression**: Researchable upgrades for range and movement.

### 4. New Building: Supply Depot
- **Requirements**: Must be researched first.
- **Cost/Upkeep**: High cost + Upkeep (-3 iron, oil, wood, food per turn).
- **Construction**: Takes 4 turns to build.
- **Durability**: Very fragile.
- **Functionality**: 
  - Provides supply on isolated road networks.
  - Radius: 2 tiles.
  - Can train Engineers & Recon units.

### 5. AI Behavior
- Must respect supply constraints.
- Must proactively build and use trucks, ships, and depots.
- Should avoid running units out of supply unless it's a strategic necessity.
